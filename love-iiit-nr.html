Introduction

Dr. Shyama Prasad Mukherjee International Institute of Information Technology Naya Raipur (IIIT-NR) was established in 2015 as a joint venture between the Chhattisgarh State Government and NTPC Limited. The institute has grown exponentially in academics, research, sports, and cultural activities.

Academics and Campus Life

IIIT-NR offers a unique academic structure with practical implementation emphasized from the first semester. Students are encouraged to work on projects and research, fostering innovation and industry readiness. Additionally, campus life is enriched with cultural fests, technical expos, and sporting events. In the rapidly evolving landscape of technology and education, the International Institute of Information Technology, Naya Raipur (IIIT-NR) stands out with its forward-thinking curriculum designed to prepare students for the future. The recent curriculum restructuring at IIIT-NR, incorporating concepts like 'Fractal Academics' and 'Skill Orientation,' positions it as a leader in innovative education. Originating from Carnegie Mellon University, the idea of 'Fractal Academics' has been embraced by some of the new IITs in India and is now a cornerstone of IIIT-NR's curriculum. This innovative approach involves offering courses with smaller credits, as minimal as one credit, unlike traditional systems that bundle large credits into single courses. This flexibility allows students to explore emerging industry-related subjects and provides a balanced mix of breadth and depth of knowledge through a T-Education model.

At IIIT-NR, the curriculum is designed with a dual focus: job orientation and research enabling. The aim is to make students industry-ready while fostering an environment that encourages undergraduate research. The senate at IIIT-NR has proposed a curriculum that supports students in undertaking research-oriented problems during their minor and major project work, enhancing their exposure to real-world science and technology challenges.

IIIT Naya Raipur’s curriculum exemplifies a perfect blend of academics, research, and industry engagement, making it a leading institution in higher education. By continually learning and adapting, IIIT-NR ensures its students are well-prepared to meet the fast-changing demands of the IT industry. With its innovative approach, the institute not only focuses on academic excellence but also fosters an entrepreneurial spirit among its students, preparing them to become the leaders of tomorrow.

Opportunities and Placements

IIIT-NR boasts strong placement records with top companies like Codenation, American Express, Deloitte, and more participating. Students are offered diverse roles, including Software Development, Data Analytics, and Business Consulting. IIIT Naya Raipur has a strong track record of ensuring 100% placements for its students. The institute's dedicated Training and Placement Cell plays a crucial role in this success. The cell organizes placement drives annually, conducts mock interviews, and provides training sessions to help students prepare for the selection process.

In recent years, IIIT-NR has seen impressive placement statistics. For instance, in 2022, the highest package offered was INR 57 LPA, with an average package of INR 12.67 LPA. Top recruiters included renowned companies like Juspay, Deloitte, Capgemini, American Express, IBM, Accenture, and Bigbasket. The institute's focus on both job orientation and research enables students to be industry-ready while also fostering an entrepreneurial spirit.

The commitment to providing high industry linkages and a curriculum that emphasizes skill development ensures that students are well-prepared to meet the demands of the fast-changing IT industry. This holistic approach to education and placement has made IIIT Naya Raipur a preferred destination for aspiring engineers.

Camaraderie and Community

The camaraderie among students and supportive seniors makes IIIT-NR feel like a home away from home. Events like Hack-a-sol, Technovate, and Aarambh highlight the institute's vibrant community spirit.
